SCENE 0 — OPENING   (target 11s)

This is Xefe — payroll, people, and accounts built for Timor-Leste. Open it,
and the work that matters is already waiting for you.


SCENE 1 — PEOPLE AND TIME   (target 17s)

Your team is in one place. Attendance, overtime, leave, and next week's shifts
stay connected, so you are not copying the same names and hours between
spreadsheets. You see what needs attention, and move on.


SCENE 2 — PAYROLL   (target 24s)

At payday, choose the period and Xefe does the careful work. It brings in
hours, applies overtime and night-work rates, then calculates social security
and withholding tax for every employee. Review the numbers, get a second
approval, and each person receives a clear payslip.


SCENE 3 — THE ENGINE   (target 18s)

That simple flow is backed by Timor-Leste's rules, written into the calculation
engine. Every line can be traced from salary and hours to deductions and net
pay — so the answer is not just fast; it is explainable.


SCENE 4 — FILE AND PAY   (target 20s)

Xefe then prepares the monthly tax and social-security forms, tracks the
deadlines, and builds the bank pack in the format local banks expect. File,
pay, and keep the evidence together — without rebuilding the month in Excel.


SCENE 5 — ONE SET OF BOOKS   (target 19s)

Money coming in and going out stays connected too. Invoices, bills, expenses,
payroll journals, and the trial balance all update one set of books, balanced
to the cent. Export the detail when your accountant or donor needs it.


SCENE 6 — WITHIN REACH   (target 20s)

Need an answer? Ask XefeBot in plain language. Managers can use Xefe from a
phone, and staff carry payslips, leave, and shifts in Ekipa. The whole system
switches between Tetun, English, and Portuguese.


SCENE 7 — TRUST   (target 15s)

Two people approve payroll. Changes are logged. The books refuse to go out of
balance. Xefe keeps the controls strong while the screen stays simple.


SCENE 8 — CLOSE   (target 11s)

Xefe. Your back office, built for Timor-Leste. Free to set up and explore.
Start today, at xefe dot T L.
