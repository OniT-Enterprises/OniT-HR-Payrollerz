## Executive Summary

OniT HR/Payroll is a comprehensive, cloud-based human resources and payroll management system built specifically for the Timor-Leste market. Unlike generic international HR software, OniT is designed from the ground up to handle TL's unique tax requirements, labor laws, banking systems, and multilingual workforce.

The platform enables businesses of any size to manage their entire employee lifecycle - from hiring to retirement - while ensuring full compliance with Timorese regulations including INSS contributions, withholding tax, and foreign worker permit tracking.

### Key Differentiators

- **Built for Timor-Leste**: Native support for TL tax rates, INSS, local banks, and mobile money
- **Trilingual**: Full support for English, Portuguese, and Tetum
- **Offline Capable**: Works with intermittent internet connectivity
- **All-in-One**: HR, Payroll, Time Tracking, Invoicing, and Accounting in one system
- **Compliance Ready**: Automated ATTL and INSS monthly reports
- **Multi-Tenant**: Can serve multiple organizations from a single deployment

## Timor-Leste Specific Features

### Tax Compliance

#### INSS (Social Security)
- Automatic calculation of employee contribution: **4%** of gross salary
- Automatic calculation of employer contribution: **6%** of gross salary
- Monthly INSS report generation in government-required format
- INSS number tracking per employee

#### Withholding Tax (Imposto sobre Rendimentos)
- Progressive tax rate calculation per TL tax law
- Tax brackets automatically applied
- ATTL monthly withholding tax report
- Annual tax certificate generation

#### Sales Tax
- 10% Imposto sobre Vendas on invoices
- Tax-inclusive and tax-exclusive pricing options
- Tax summary reports

### Local Banking Integration

#### Supported Banks
- **BNU** (Banco Nacional Ultramarino) - File format for bulk transfers
- **BNCTL** (Banco Nacional de Comércio de Timor-Leste) - Payment file generation
- **Mandiri** - Indonesian bank format support
- **ANZ** - International format support

#### Mobile Money
- **Telkomcel** mobile money integration ready
- **Telemor** mobile money integration ready
- Pay employees directly to mobile wallets
- Accept customer payments via mobile money

### Language Support

- **English** - Full interface translation
- **Portuguese** - Full interface translation
- **Tetum** - Full interface translation (first HR system with Tetum support)
- Language switching without logout
- Bilingual document generation (payslips, contracts, reports)

### TL Public Holidays

Pre-configured Timor-Leste public holidays:
- New Year's Day (1 January)
- Veterans Day (3 March)
- Good Friday (variable)
- Restoration of Independence Day (20 May)
- Corpus Christi (variable)
- Popular Consultation Day (30 August)
- Independence Day (28 November)
- National Heroes Day (7 December)
- Our Lady of Immaculate Conception (8 December)
- Christmas Day (25 December)

Automatic holiday detection for payroll and scheduling.

### Foreign Worker Compliance

- Work permit tracking with expiry alerts
- Visa type recording
- Nationality-based document requirements
- Foreign worker register for SEPFOPE reporting
- Automatic flagging of expiring permits
- Compliance dashboard for HR

### TL Labor Law Support

- Overtime calculation per Labor Code:
  - Weekday overtime: 1.5x
  - Weekend/holiday: 2x
- Minimum wage tracking
- Probation period management
- Contract type handling (fixed-term, permanent)
- Notice period calculation
- Severance calculation
