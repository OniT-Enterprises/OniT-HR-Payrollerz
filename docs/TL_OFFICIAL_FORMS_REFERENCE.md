## WIT (Wage Income Tax) Requirements

### Tax Rates
**Residents** (183+ days in TL):
- $0 - $500/month: 0%
- Over $500/month: 10% of amount over $500

**Non-Residents**:
- Flat 10% on total wages (no threshold)

### Filing Requirements

#### Monthly WIT
- **Due Date**: 15th of the following month
- **Submission**: 3 copies of Monthly Consolidated Tax Form to BNU bank
- **Payment**: Due with form submission

#### Annual WIT
- **Due Date**: March 31 following tax year
- **Form**: Employer Wage Income Tax Withholding Form
- **Submission**: 3 copies with payment to BNU bank

### Employer Obligations
1. Withhold WIT from employee wages each month
2. Submit monthly consolidated tax form by 15th
3. File annual employer WIT return by March 31
4. Provide wage statements to employees on request or at year-end

### Work Visa (Type C) Requirements
- **Duration**: Up to 1 year
- **Fee**: $50 USD
- **Processing**: ~15 business days
- **Cannot be applied for on arrival**

### Required Documents for Work Visa
1. Valid passport (6+ months validity)
2. Employment offer/contract
3. Proof of accommodation
4. Return ticket or proof of funds for departure
5. Medical certificate
6. Police clearance
7. Means of subsistence documentation

## INSS (Social Security) - segurancasocial.gov.tl

### Official Portal
- **Main Site**: https://segurancasocial.gov.tl/ (Note: May be intermittently available)

### Contribution Rates (as of 2025)
- **Employer**: 6% of gross salary
- **Employee**: 4% of gross salary (withheld by employer)
- **Total**: 10%

### Small Employer Reduction (until 2026)
Employers with 10 or fewer workers (60% Timorese) may qualify for reduced rates.

### Filing Requirements
- **Declaration**: Monthly for each employee
- **Due Date**: 10th of the following month
- **Summary**: Monthly summary required with individual declarations

### Foreign Worker Exemption
Foreign workers covered by social security in their home country may be exempt for up to 10 years (with proof).

### Consolidated Monthly Taxes Form Structure

The official form is titled "CONSOLIDATED MONTHLY TAXES FORM" from the National Directorate of Domestic Revenue.

#### Header Fields
| Field | Description | Format |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Month | Reporting month | 01-12 |
| Year | Tax year | YYYY |
| TIN | Taxpayer Identification Number | Numeric |
| Taxpayer Name | Company legal name | Text |
| Establishment Name | Trading name (if different) | Text |

#### Section 1: WAGE INCOME TAX
| Line | Field | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 5 | Total gross wages paid during the month | Sum of all employee gross wages (USD, no cents) |
| 10 | Total Wages Income Tax (withheld during the month) | Sum of WIT deducted from all employees |

#### Section 2: WITHHOLDING TAX
| Line | Payment Type | Rate |
|------|--------------|------|
| 45/50 | Prizes and Lotteries | 10% |
| 55/60 | Royalties | 10% |
| 65/70 | Rent land and buildings | 10% |
| 75/80 | Construction and building activities | 2% |
| 85/90 | Construction consulting services | 4% |
| 95/100 | Mining and mining support services | 4% |
| 105/110 | Transportation - Air and Sea | 2.64% |
| 115/120 | Non-resident without permanent establishment | 10% (all payment types) |
| 130 | TOTAL WITHHOLDING TAX | Sum of lines 50-120 |

#### Section 3: SERVICES TAX
| Line | Service Type | Rate |
|------|--------------|------|
| 15 | Hotel services | 5% |
| 20 | Restaurant and bar services | 5% |
| 30 | Telecommunications services | 5% |
| 35 | Total Sales (before tax) | Sum of 15+20+30 |
| 40 | Services Tax Payable | Line 35 × 5% |

#### Section 4: ANNUAL INCOME TAX INSTALLMENT
| Payer Type | Due Dates | Rate |
|------------|-----------|------|
| Quarterly (turnover ≤ $1M) | 15th Apr, Jul, Oct + 15th Jan following | 0.5% of turnover |
| Monthly (turnover > $1M) | 15th of each month from Feb | 0.5% of turnover |

#### Section 5: PAYMENT ADVICE
| Tax Type | Amount Source | BNU Account |
|----------|---------------|-------------|
| Wages Tax | Line 10 | 286442.10.001 |
| Withholding Tax | Line 130 | 286830.10.001 |
| Services Tax | Line 40 | 286636.10.001 |
| Income Tax Installment | Line 20 | 286539.10.001 |
| **TOTAL PAID** | Sum of above | - |

#### Section 6: DECLARATION
| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Full Name | Authorized signatory name |
| Date | Submission date |
| Telephone | Contact number |
| Signature | Physical signature |

### Data Format Requirements
- **Currency**: All amounts in USD
- **Decimal places**: Do not show cents (whole dollars only)
- **Month format**: 01-12
- **Year format**: YYYY
